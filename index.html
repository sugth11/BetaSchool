Welcome to Beta International School

Welcome to Beta International School (BIS), a world class school in Chennai that aims at setting a benchmark for progressive and holistic education in India. At Beta International School (BIS), learning becomes fun through various interactive sessions and activities. Our school follows CBSE curriculum and IGCSE Program that makes use of modern teaching methodologies achieved through smart classrooms and labs with in-built audio-visual systems.
